FAQ

  • How do I create a memory? — Tap the + button on the Memories screen and fill in your trip details.
  • How do I share a memory? — Open a memory and tap the share icon in the top right corner.
  • How do I restore my purchase? — Go to Settings → Upgrade to Premium → Restore Purchase.
  • Can I use the app offline? — Yes. Your journal remains available offline. Maps, weather, purchases, and iCloud Backup need a network connection.
  • How do I delete my data? — Delete the app to remove local data. If iCloud Backup is enabled, turn it off and choose Delete iCloud Backup in Settings to remove the recovery copy too.
  • How do I set a cover photo? — In the memory edit screen, tap any photo to set it as the cover.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Overview

Memora Trips is a privacy-first, offline-first application. Your journal is encrypted on your device. Optional features use protected device data only with your permission, and optional iCloud Backup stores a recovery copy in your private iCloud container.

Data Collection

Memora does not operate account, analytics, advertising, or tracking servers. The app contacts service providers only when needed for a feature you use. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No sale of personal data

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data in its encrypted local database:

  • Trip memories (titles, dates, locations, notes, ratings)
  • Photos you attach to memories
  • Planned trips
  • App preferences and settings

iCloud Backup

If you enable iCloud Backup, Memora stores a recovery copy of your memories, plans, photos, ratings, notes, activity summaries, and settings in the app's private iCloud container. This copy is controlled through your Apple ID and is not available to Cerberus Software Technologies. You can disable backup, keep the existing copy, or delete the iCloud backup from Memora Settings.

Calendar Data

Calendar access is optional and is requested only after you choose Scan My Calendar. Memora reads event titles, dates, locations, and notes to identify possible trips. Scanning and matching happen on your device. Scanned events are not sent to our servers, Memora never changes your calendar, and Memora does not access or change reminders.

No suggestions are selected automatically. Only trips you explicitly choose are saved to Memora. Saved trip details may be included in your private iCloud backup when backup is enabled.

Apple Health and Activity

With your permission, Memora reads step counts and cycling distance for your trip dates. Memora does not write to Apple Health. Activity summaries saved with a memory remain local unless you enable iCloud Backup or explicitly export or share them.

Location Data

The app may request location permission to:

  • Tag your current location when creating a memory
  • Detect arrival at planned trip destinations (using region monitoring)

Saved memory and plan locations are stored in the encrypted local database and may be included in your private iCloud backup. Map, geocoding, and weather providers may receive the coordinates or search text needed to answer a request.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps / Places API — Used for location search and map display. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo — Receives the coordinates and dates needed to return weather information.
  • Apple iCloud — Stores an optional recovery copy in the app's private iCloud container when you enable iCloud Backup. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit / Google Play Billing — Used for in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's and Google's privacy policies.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app removes its local data. If iCloud Backup is enabled, the private recovery copy can remain after uninstalling so you can restore it later. To remove it before uninstalling, go to Memora Settings, turn off iCloud Backup, and choose Delete iCloud Backup. You can also manage stored app data through your Apple iCloud storage controls.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information.
