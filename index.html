FAQ

  • How do I create a memory? — Tap the + button on the Memories screen and fill in your trip details.
  • How do I share a memory? — Open a memory, scroll to the bottom, and tap the Share button.
  • How do I restore my purchase? — Go to Settings → Upgrade to Premium → Restore Purchase.
  • Can I use the app offline? — Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. No internet required.
  • How do I delete my data? — Delete the app from your device. All data is stored locally and will be removed.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Overview

Memora Trips is a privacy-first, offline-first application. Your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data shared with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Trip memories (titles, dates, locations, notes, ratings)
  • Photos you attach to memories
  • Planned trips
  • App preferences and settings

Location Data

The app may request location permission to:

  • Auto-detect your current location when creating a memory
  • Detect arrival at planned trip destinations (background location)

Location data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps / Places API — Used for location search and map display. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo — Used for weather data. No personal data is sent; only coordinates and dates.
  • Apple StoreKit — Used for in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently removes all data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
